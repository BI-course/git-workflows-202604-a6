# Data Governance Overview

Data governance is the framework of policies, processes, roles, and standards
that ensure data is managed as a trusted, secure, and valuable organizational
asset. It defines how data is collected, stored, accessed, used, and maintained
across its lifecycle.

Understanding data governance is critical for building reliable Business
Intelligence (BI) systems, ensuring regulatory compliance, and enabling
consistent, high-quality analytics.

## Core Components of Data Governance

### 1. Data Ownership and Stewardship
Data governance establishes clear accountability by assigning data owners and
stewards responsible for data quality, definition, and usage.

**Key Responsibilities:**
- Defining data standards and business rules
- Approving data access and usage
- Resolving data quality issues

### 2. Data Quality Management
This focuses on maintaining accurate, complete, consistent, and timely data
across systems.

**Key Areas:**
- Data validation and cleansing
- Monitoring data accuracy and completeness
- Handling duplicates and inconsistencies

### 3. Data Security and Privacy
Data governance enforces controls to protect sensitive and confidential data
from unauthorized access and misuse.

**Key Practices:**
- Role-based access control
- Data classification and masking
- Compliance with privacy and regulatory requirements

### 4. Metadata Management
Metadata provides context about data, including definitions, lineage, and
usage, enabling better understanding and trust in BI outputs.

**Examples:**
- Business glossaries
- Data lineage documentation
- Dataset ownership records

### 5. Policies, Standards, and Compliance
Governance defines rules for how data should be handled across departments and
ensures adherence to internal policies and external regulations.

**Examples:**
- Data retention policies
- Naming conventions
- Audit and compliance requirements

## Why Data Governance Matters
Effective data governance:
- Improves trust in reports and dashboards
- Reduces data-related risks
- Enables consistent decision-making
- Supports scalable and compliant BI architectures
